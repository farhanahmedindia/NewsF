Email phishing attacks against Indian businesses

Who this matters to: Businesses in India that rely on email.

What happened

Attackers sent emails that looked legitimate. When clicked, they gained remote access to systems.

Why this matters

Email-based attacks are cheap, effective, and often go unnoticed until damage is done.

What usually fails

  • No MFA on email accounts
  • Lack of employee awareness
  • Over-trusting email links

What you should do

  • Enable MFA for all email accounts
  • Train staff to avoid unknown links
  • Block risky attachments
