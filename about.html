About RiskBrief

RiskBrief translates complex security news into plain language for founders and small teams. We cut through the technical jargon to deliver what business leaders need to know.

Our Mission

Security information is often written by technical experts for technical experts. This leaves business leaders vulnerable because they can't quickly assess risks or take appropriate action.

RiskBrief bridges this gap by providing:

  • Clear explanations of security threats
  • Business-focused impact analysis
  • Actionable recommendations for non-technical teams
  • Regular updates on emerging threats

Time-Saving

We research and summarize security news so you can stay informed in minutes, not hours.

Risk Reduction

Our actionable recommendations help you address vulnerabilities before they become problems.

Plain Language

No technical jargon or acronyms – just clear explanations you can understand and act on.

Our Methodology

Each RiskBrief follows a rigorous process:

  1. We monitor multiple security sources for emerging threats
  2. We assess business impact and relevance to small organizations
  3. We translate technical details into business risks
  4. We provide prioritized, actionable recommendations
  5. We review and update information as threats evolve

Who We Serve

RiskBrief is designed for:

  • Startup founders without dedicated security teams
  • Small business owners managing their own technology
  • Non-technical executives responsible for business risk
  • Anyone who needs to understand security without a technical background
